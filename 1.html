WATER (H2O)
Water is an inorganic compound with the chemical formula H2O. It is a transparent, tasteless, odorless, and nearly colorless chemical substance, and it is the main constituent of Earth's stratosphere hydrosphere and the fluids of all known living organisms. It is vital for all known forms of life, despite not providing food energy, or organic micronutrients. The hydrogen atoms are attached to the oxygen atom at an angle of 104.45o. "Water" is also the name of the liquid state of H2O at standard temperature and pressure.
